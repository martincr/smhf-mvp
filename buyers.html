Buyer FAQ

How do buyers receive leads?

For the MVP, you can send email lead cards or simple dashboard notifications. Keep it lightweight until you see closed deals.

What is in the lead card?

ZIP or address, city, state, seller timeline, condition, short seller notes, and contact path. Add photos when available.

How should offers work?

The simplest version is a reply flow: buyer submits a price range, close speed, and a request for more info if needed.
