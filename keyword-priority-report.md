# SMHF keyword and MVP notes

## What the uploaded keyword files suggest

- The head term **sell my house fast** has the biggest volume in the upload, but it is labeled primarily informational.
- The stronger commercial / transactional cluster is around **for cash**, **as is**, **now**, and **near me** queries.
- State pages are viable, but the first rollout should focus on states where you can actually fulfill demand with buyers.

## MVP positioning

- Treat the first version as a lead marketplace, not a full iBuyer.
- Use AI for seller intake cleanup, lead scoring, summary generation, and buyer routing.
- Prefer seller uploads over image scraping.
- Keep the first form short.
